About me

Former Geologist turned Software Engineer.

I originally studied Geology, Marine Science, and Art History at Rutgers University New Brunswick. After graduating, I began working as a Geologist in the environmental field. Finding myself not satisfied with non-academic geology, I looked for a new calling.

So while working as a geologist, I went back to school and got a second Bachelors in Computer Science. From there, I worked at BAE Systems as a Software Engineer and now at CACI as a Software Engineer and Network Security Engineer.

As of recently, I have also released my first mobile app the on the Play Store titled WaterMe! Follow the project link to check it out!
